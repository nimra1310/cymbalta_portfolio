Careers

Cymbalta is a progressive leader in crop protection and agricultural innovation. We are committed to empowering farmers with trusted solutions, sustainable practices, and reliable support that drive higher yields and a brighter future for Pakistan’s agriculture.Through cutting-edge technology and collaborative partnerships, we aim to revolutionize farming and contribute to the economic growth of our nation

Position: Venture Architect

Locations: Multan, Lahore, Islamabad
Full-time

Role Summary

As a Venture Architect, you will be at the forefront of our innovation initiatives, ideating and executing new business ventures from scratch. This unique role is ideal for ambitious individuals who thrive in a start-up environment, with the resources and support of a well-established corporation. You will have the autonomy to shape projects, hire teams, and launch new companies around your ideas.

Responsibilities

  • Identify and develop new venture opportunities aligned with market trends and organizational goals.
  • Conduct market research, business model design, and financial forecasting to validate business ideas.
  • Build, lead, and inspire a small team for each venture, ensuring accountability and results.
  • Drive project development from ideation to market launch with high levels of independence.
  • Collaborate with our leadership team, leveraging organizational resources as needed.

Qualifications

  • Recent graduate (Class of 2016-2024) with a degree in business, technology, or a related field.
  • Demonstrated experience in innovation, project management, or entrepreneurship.
  • Excellent analytical, strategic thinking, and problem-solving skills.
  • Ability to thrive under autonomy and take ownership of projects from start to finish.

What We Offer

  • Competitive compensation package and performance-based incentives.
  • Access to industry experts, mentors, and resources for developing and scaling your ideas.
  • A rare opportunity to work in a dynamic environment where your ideas can become reality.

Application Process

To apply, please send your resume and a brief cover letter describing a venture idea you'd like to pursue to the relevant email address given below:

Email: jobs@suncropgroup.com
