Welcome to Cymbalta Pakistan

Dedicated to creating sustainable agricultural solutions for farmers and communities across Pakistan.

Our Story

Cymbalta Pakistan is not just a company—it’s a trusted leader in agricultural innovation, dedicated to transforming the future of farming. Over the past three consecutive years, we’ve been driving positive change in Pakistan’s agrochemical industry by providing effective, science-based solutions that boost farm productivity and protect crops. Our commitment to quality, sustainability, and farmer success is at the heart of everything we do, shaping a brighter agricultural future for all.

Our mission is to enhance agricultural productivity and food security across the region by delivering high-quality inputs and services to farmers. Through our comprehensive approach, we aim to transform the agricultural landscape and improve the livelihoods of farming communities.

Our Values

Innovation

We constantly seek new and better ways to improve agricultural practices, leveraging technology and research to develop sustainable solutions.

Integrity

We conduct our business with honesty, transparency, and accountability, building trust with all our stakeholders.

Impact

We measure our success by the positive difference we make in farmers' lives and the communities we serve.

We strive for the highest standards in all our operations, products, and services to deliver maximum value to our customers.

Our Leadership

Cymbalta Pakistan is led by a dedicated team of professionals with extensive experience in agriculture, business development, and sustainability.

Shahid Malik

Founder & CEO

With over 25 years of experience in the agricultural sector, Shahid Malik founded cymbalta Pakistan with a vision to empower Pakistani farmers through access to quality inputs and knowledge. His leadership has been instrumental in expanding the company's operations across multiple countries and establishing partnerships with key stakeholders in the agricultural value chain.

Under his guidance, cymbalta Pakistan has become a leading provider of agricultural solutions, committed to sustainable farming practices and food security in Pakistan.

Our Impact

At Sun Crop Group, we believe that improving agricultural productivity is key to addressing food security challenges in Africa. By providing farmers with quality inputs, technical expertise, and market access, we have helped thousands of farmers increase their yields and incomes.

Our work extends beyond commercial interests to embrace environmental sustainability and community development. Through various initiatives, we promote sustainable farming practices, water conservation, and soil health management to ensure the long-term viability of agricultural systems.

We are proud of the positive impact we have made on the lives of farmers and communities across the regions we serve, and we remain committed to expanding our reach and deepening our impact in the years to come.
