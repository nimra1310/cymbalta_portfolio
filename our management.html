OUR LEADERSHIP

At Cymbalta Pakistan, our leadership is driven by vision, integrity, and a deep commitment to transforming agriculture. Guided by dynamic leaders with years of experience in the agrochemical industry, we combine strategic foresight with hands-on expertise to deliver innovation, empower farmers, and build enduring partnerships. Our leadership team is dedicated to creating lasting impact—driving growth, setting new benchmarks, and shaping a brighter agricultural future for Pakistan.

Shahid Malik
Founder & CEO

Shahid Malik is the dynamic CEO of Cymbalta Pakistan, a progressive agrochemical company transforming the future of agriculture in Pakistan. With deep expertise in sales, marketing, and agribusiness leadership, he has been instrumental in positioning Cymbalta Pakistan as a trusted partner for farmers and dealers nationwide. Under his dynamic leadership, Cymbalta Pakistan has grown its nationwide presence, launched cutting-edge crop protection solutions, and fostered a powerful network of partners committed to farmer success. With a clear focus on innovation, sustainability, and farmer-first values, Shahid Malik is steering the company toward its vision protecting crops, enhancing yields, and shaping a brighter agricultural future for Pakistan.

Muqaddas Malik
Director

Muqaddas Malik- A distinguished Member of the Board of Directors at Cymbalta Pakistan, plays a vital role in steering the company’s strategic direction and ensuring its continued growth and success. With a strong sense of responsibility and forward-looking vision, she actively contributes to shaping policies, enhancing governance, and guiding key decisions that strengthen Cymbalta Pakistan’s position as a progressive agrochemical company.Her leadership is marked by resilience, dedication, and a commitment to excellence. As part of the Board, she not only ensures alignment with the company’s mission but also focuses on long-term sustainability and farmer-centric progress. By combining insight with determination, Ms. Muqaddas Malik continues to add immense value to Cymbalta Pakistan’s journey of innovation, trust, and agricultural transformation. As a symbol of women’s leadership in agriculture and corporate governance, she stands as an inspiration—proving that vision, determination, and purpose know no boundaries.
