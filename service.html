OUR SERVICES

At Cymbalta Pakistan, we don’t just provide agrochemical products — we deliver end-to-end agricultural solutions that empower farmers, support dealers, and strengthen Pakistan’s agricultural future. Our services combine expert guidance, uncompromising quality, and continuous innovation to create long-term value for all stakeholders.

Comprehensive Agricultural Solutions

At Corteva AgriScience, we provide expert services tailored to meet the diverse needs of modern farming. Our team of specialists works closely with farmers to optimize crop production, ensure quality, and drive innovation in agricultural practices.

Field Advisory Service

At Cymbalta Pakistan, our Field Advisory Services are designed to empower farmers with expert guidance, practical solutions, and real-time support throughout the growing season. Backed by a team of skilled agronomists, we provide personalized recommendations that help farmers maximize crop yields, reduce risks, and adopt sustainable farming practices.

We recognize that every farm faces unique conditions and challenges. That’s why our advisors work hand in hand with farmers to develop customized strategies that ensure healthier crops, stronger soil, and more profitable harvests.

Crop Monitoring

Regular field visits and crop scouting to detect issues early and protect yields.

Pest Management

Integrated pest management (IPM) solutions to safeguard crops while minimizing chemical usage.

Soil Analysis

Comprehensive soil testing with tailored fertilization programs for improved productivity.

Technology Integration

Guidance on adopting precision agriculture tools for efficiency and smarter farming..

Quality Assurance

At Cymbalta Pakistan, quality is more than a standard—it’s a promise. Our Quality Assurance services ensure that every agricultural product we deliver meets the highest benchmarks of safety, reliability, and performance. Through rigorous testing protocols and strict quality control measures, we safeguard the integrity of products at every stage of the supply chain.

From procurement to packaging, our dedicated quality assurance team works relentlessly to maintain product excellence, helping farmers and partners build trust, access premium markets, and achieve long-term success.

Product Testing

Comprehensive laboratory testing to ensure compliance with regulatory standards and quality benchmarks.

Certification Support

Assistance in obtaining organic, sustainable, and specialized farming certifications.

Quality Management

Strong systems to maintain consistent product standards across every batch.

Traceability Solutions

Advanced tracking for full transparency from production to the farmer’s field.

Research & Development

At Cymbalta Pakistan, our Research & Development (R&D) service is the driving force behind innovation in agriculture. We invest in cutting-edge research, advanced technologies, and field-driven insights to create practical solutions that address the evolving challenges of modern farming.

Through collaborations with leading research institutions and continuous field trials, we develop products and methodologies that not only improve crop productivity but also promote sustainability and environmental stewardship.

Varietal Development

Research into new crop varieties with improved yield potential, disease resistance, and climate adaptability.

Sustainable Solutions

Field Trials

Technology Innovation

Precision agriculture tools and digital technologies that make farming smarter, more efficient, and more profitable.
