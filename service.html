OUR SERVICES

Expert agricultural services designed to maximize your farm's potential and ensure sustainable growth

Comprehensive Agricultural Solutions

At Corteva AgriScience, we provide expert services tailored to meet the diverse needs of modern farming. Our team of specialists works closely with farmers to optimize crop production, ensure quality, and drive innovation in agricultural practices.

Field Advisory Service

Our Field Advisory Service provides personalized guidance and support to farmers throughout the growing season. Our experienced agronomists help you make informed decisions to maximize crop yields while maintaining sustainable farming practices.

We understand that each farm has unique challenges and opportunities, which is why our advisors work closely with you to develop customized solutions that address your specific needs.

Crop Monitoring

Regular field visits to assess crop health and identify potential issues before they impact yields.

Pest Management

Integrated pest management strategies to protect your crops while minimizing chemical usage.

Soil Analysis

Comprehensive soil testing and recommendations for optimal fertilization programs.

Technology Integration

Guidance on implementing precision agriculture technologies for improved efficiency.

Quality Assurance

Our Quality Assurance service ensures that your agricultural products meet the highest standards of quality and safety. We implement rigorous testing protocols and quality control measures throughout the production process.

From seed selection to harvest, our quality assurance team works diligently to maintain the integrity of your products, helping you gain access to premium markets and build consumer trust.

Product Testing

Comprehensive laboratory testing to ensure products meet regulatory standards and quality benchmarks.

Certification Support

Assistance with obtaining relevant certifications for organic, sustainable, and other specialized farming practices.

Quality Management

Implementation of quality management systems to maintain consistent product standards.

Traceability Solutions

Advanced tracking systems to ensure complete traceability from farm to consumer.

Research & Development

Our Research & Development service drives innovation in agricultural practices and technologies. We invest in cutting-edge research to develop new solutions that address the evolving challenges faced by farmers worldwide.

Through collaborative partnerships with leading research institutions and continuous field trials, we create innovative products and methodologies that enhance agricultural productivity while promoting environmental sustainability.

Varietal Development

Research into new crop varieties with improved yield potential, disease resistance, and climate adaptability.

Sustainable Solutions

Development of eco-friendly farming methods that reduce environmental impact while maintaining productivity.

Field Trials

Extensive testing of new products and techniques under various field conditions to ensure efficacy.

Technology Innovation

Creation of new digital tools and technologies to enhance precision farming capabilities.

Partner With Us For Agricultural Excellence

Whether you're looking to optimize your current farming operations, ensure product quality, or implement innovative solutions, our team of experts is ready to support your agricultural journey.
