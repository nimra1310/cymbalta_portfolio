Featured Product

Wrestler

Wrestler 12% SC is a powerful dual-action insecticide designed to deliver rapid knockdown and long-lasting protection against major sucking and chewing insects. With both systemic and contact movement, it penetrates deep into plant tissues, ensuring effective control even on hidden pests. Its combination of active ingredients targets the insect nervous system, providing strong residual control and promoting healthier crops.

Product Information

Brand Name: Wrestler 12% SC

Active Ingredients:Abamectin 1.8% + Thiamethoxam 10.2% Recommended Crops: Cotton, vegetables, chilies, orchards Target Pests: Aphids, jassids, thrips, mites, whiteflies, leaf miners, and other sucking insects Mode of Action: Systemic, contact, and translaminar activity with neurotoxic action. Pack Size: 400 ml.
